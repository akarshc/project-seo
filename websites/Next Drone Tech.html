What are Drones – UAV, UAS, Quadcopter Definition

Drones are of many kinds with distinct names – UAV, UAS are few examples. Actually, the Drone is an unmanned aircraft which is controlled by a remote or an onboard computer.

What are drones? Above paragraphs defines you the answer.

The definition of UAV is an unmanned aerial vehicle and UAS is unmanned aerial systems – where you can find same features of drones with distinct terms. Model aircraft and non-model aircraft are the two classifications of the drones.

Model Aircraft

If you’d like to take a test with drones, this is the right choice. Why? A perfect hobby partner. Like the name says, Model aircrafts ain’t capable of handling non-hobby purpose, it is illegal.

Multicopter

Model aircraft which uses multiple rotors are Multicopters. Concerning drones, an onboard computer is furthermore taken in controlling Multicopter.

So, want to know how it comes to be a UAV/drone? When the Multicopter is capable of autonomous flights, it becomes drones. GPS data and barometer connects with the accelerometer and gyro information to form an autonomous flight.

Quadcopter

Quadrotor helicopter (Quadcopter) are four rotors helicopters.

Hexacopter

When six rotors are used in Model aircraft they are Hexacopter.

Octacopter

Eight rotors are used.

Non-model Aircraft

As I’ve figured above in model aircraft that the non-model aircraft are used for professional and military works. For using the non-model aircraft, you need the registration and certification from FAA.

Terms for non-model aircraft are of the following:

  1. RPAS – Remotely Piloted Aerial System
  2. sUAS – Small Unmanned Aircraft System
  3. UAS – Unmanned Aircraft System
  4. UAV – Unmanned Aerial Vehicle
